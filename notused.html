What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is where participants work together to create awesome projects. We 'll provide the swag and experience.

How much does it cost?

It 'll cost you nothing. Just show up with a laptop and a charger, and we 'll take care of you.

Where is sjHacks?

sjHacks will occur somewhere in Silicon Valley. The venue and date have not been secured yet, but will be soon!

Who can participate?

sjHacks is open to all high school students. Sadly, we are not able to accept middle school or college students.

What should I bring?

All attendees should bring a valid form of ID. Sleeping bags and other toiletries are recommended.

Can I only "hack "?

No! We 'll have sections designated for attendees to just chill or rest, as well as activities to keep everyone occupied.

What if i don 't have an idea?

No problem! Most people don 't have an idea when they arrive. We 'll host an activity to get you a team.

What can I build?

Anything that your heart desires. A website, an app, a robot; everything will be considered.

What if I 'd like to mentor?

We 'd love to have you! Shoot us an email at lelandhacks@gmail.com. We 'll get back to you as soon as possible.
